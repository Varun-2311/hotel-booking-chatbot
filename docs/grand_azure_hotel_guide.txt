ABOUT THE HOTEL
---------------
Grand Azure Hotel is a boutique luxury hotel in the heart of the city, with skyline views and premium hospitality.
Established in 2010, the hotel welcomes guests from over 80 countries and offers 98 rooms and suites, two restaurants,
a rooftop bar, a full-service spa, and a modern fitness centre.

Address: 14 Azure Boulevard, Downtown District
Phone: +91-80-4567-8900
Email: reservations@grandazure.com
Check-in time: 2:00 PM
Check-out time: 12:00 PM (noon)
Early check-in and late check-out are available on request, subject to availability.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
--------------------------

Q: Is breakfast included in the room rate?
A: Breakfast is not included in standard rates. It can be added for INR 850 per person per day. Some promotional packages include breakfast, so guests should ask reservations when booking.

Q: What is the check-in age requirement?
A: Guests must be 18 years or older to check in. A valid government-issued photo ID is required.

Q: Do you have accessible rooms?
A: Yes. There are 4 fully accessible rooms: 2 Standard Rooms, 1 Deluxe Room, and 1 Junior Suite. They include roll-in showers, grab bars, and lowered fixtures.

Q: Is there a gym and pool for all guests?
A: Yes. The fitness centre and both pools are complimentary for all hotel guests.

Q: Can I store luggage after check-out?
A: Yes. Complimentary luggage storage is available at the concierge desk.

Q: Do you offer long-stay discounts?
A: Yes. Stays of 7 nights or more receive a 10 percent discount. Stays of 14 nights or more receive a 15 percent discount. Guests should contact reservations for extended-stay rates.

Q: Is the hotel wheelchair accessible?
A: Yes. The hotel has step-free entrances, lifts to all floors, accessible bathrooms, and trained staff to assist guests with mobility needs.

Q: Can I request a high floor or specific view?
A: Yes. Preferences are noted and staff will do their best to accommodate them, but floor and view requests cannot be guaranteed.

Q: What time does the rooftop bar close?
A: The Rooftop Grill closes at 1:00 AM daily.

Q: Is the hotel family-friendly?
A: Yes. The hotel offers family rooms, baby cots, a kids' menu at Azure Brasserie, and babysitting services arranged through the concierge with advance notice.
